How it works

Step 1

Add your Gemini key

Paste your free Google Gemini API key once. Stored locally in Chrome — never sent anywhere.

Step 2

Open any page

Navigate to an article, blog post, research paper, or YouTube video.

Step 3

Click the Briefer icon

The side panel opens and your summary appears in seconds — no copy-pasting required.

Step 4

Browse your history

Every summary is saved automatically and grouped by topic — your personal knowledge base.
